True said a smoking gentleman whose coat had the fine polish about the collar
elbows seams and shoulderblades that longcontinued friction with grimy surfaces
will produce and which is usually more desired on furniture than on clothes. From his
appearance he had possibly been in former time groom or coachman to some
neighbouring county family. Ive had my breedings in as good circles I may say as
any man he added and I know true cultivation or nobody do and I can declare
shes got it in the bone mind ye I say as much as any female in the fair though it
may want a little bringing out. Then crossing his legs he resumed his pipe with a
nicelyadjusted gaze at a point in the air.
